# Product Vision

Reading important documents online is often slow, fragile, and discouraging. Long texts—court opinions, research papers, technical essays—require sustained attention, but the web constantly interrupts that attention with questions, tangents, and the need to look things up. When readers leave the document to answer a question, they often lose their place, their momentum, or even their motivation to continue.

The goal of this product is to make deep reading resilient to curiosity.

Instead of treating digressions as distractions, the system embraces them and organizes them. A reader can follow questions wherever they lead, knowing they can always return to the original thread of reading.

---

## Incremental Reading

When a user imports a document, the system prepares it for incremental reading.

Rather than presenting the document as a single long text, it is broken into small, logical chunks—typically short enough to read in five to ten minutes. These chunks are placed into a queue so the reader can make steady progress through large materials without needing a large block of time.

At any point, the reader can choose:

- the next chunk from a particular document, or
- whatever chunk is next in their overall reading queue.

This transforms large texts from daunting commitments into manageable steps.

---

## Curiosity Without Losing Your Place

While reading, questions inevitably arise.

A term may be unfamiliar. A concept may require background. A reference may point to another document. Normally, following these questions means leaving the text and risking losing the thread.

In this system, questions become structured explorations.

A reader can ask questions directly within the document—for example:

> “What is substantial-evidence review?”

These questions create new branches connected to the original passage. The system helps explore the answer while preserving the reader’s place. At any moment, the reader can return to the exact point where the question arose.

Curiosity becomes a feature of the reading process rather than a disruption.

---

## Mapping the Terrain of Learning

As readers follow questions and explore related ideas, the system builds a map of their intellectual path.

The document itself forms the trunk of the tree.  
Questions and explorations form branches.  
Further inquiries create smaller branches extending outward.

Over time, this produces a navigable structure showing:

- where questions originated
- how ideas connect across documents
- what paths the reader followed through the material

The interface reflects this structure visually, using branching diagrams similar to version-control trees. Readers can see their progress, revisit earlier explorations, or jump back to the main thread at any point.

---

## Turning Understanding into Memory

Insights and explanations discovered along the way can be converted into spaced-repetition flashcards.

Instead of generating flashcards in isolation, the cards arise naturally from moments of curiosity or confusion during reading. Because they originate from real questions encountered in context, they reinforce the exact ideas that were difficult or important.

Over time, the system helps transform exploration into durable knowledge.

---

## Cross-Document Discovery

As readers work through many documents, patterns begin to emerge.

Concepts encountered in one text may appear again in another. Questions asked in one context may illuminate a different document later. The system recognizes these overlaps and connects them.

This allows diversions across documents to merge into shared branches of understanding rather than remaining isolated notes.

The reader gradually builds a network of knowledge spanning multiple texts.

---

## The Core Idea

The product is built around a simple principle:

**Deep reading should be able to survive curiosity.**

Readers should be able to follow questions wherever they lead, explore ideas freely, and still maintain steady forward progress through complex material.

Instead of forcing readers to choose between focus and exploration, the system makes both possible at the same time.
